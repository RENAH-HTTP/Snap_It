SNAP IT
scan the world · make it a beat

Scan

Point the camera at everyday objects — a cup, a book, a bottle. Each one unlocks a drum sound on the keypad.

Play

Tap keypad pads to hear your sounds. Hatched pads are still hiding out in the real world.

Sequence

Every sound in your kit is already a row in the sequencer — just toggle its 16 steps. Drag the pitch lane up or down to tune single steps.

Shape

Ride the mixer crayons for level, pull the ADSR faders, and drag the BPM, SWING and KEY chips for the groove.

FX

Jam

Host or join a WiFi session and build the beat together — one machine plays the sound for the whole room.
