Point it at the world.

Aim the camera at a cup, a book, a bottle. The eye locks on and reads what it sees — all on your device.

  • Recognises 80 everyday objects
  • Each one carries its own drum sound
  • The screen fills with signal as it scans

Five effects, one pad.

Drag between the zones to blend drive, filter, crush, reverb and echo in real time — perform live sweeps over your loop.

  • Each corner is an effect
  • The space between blends them
  • Double-tap to bypass the rack

Build the beat.

Toggle 16 steps per track, tune each hit, drag a note to hold it longer, and add as many pads as you can find.

  • Infinite pads, unlimited tracks
  • Per-step pitch and note length
  • A live playhead sweeping the grid

Play together.

Host a WiFi room and build one shared loop in sync. Your name shows on everyone's screen; one machine is the speaker.

  • Host or join with a local IP
  • Everyone edits the same project
  • Log in to keep your kit, or just pick a name
